Ingredients

Serving Size 2 slices

  • Flatbread
  • Tomato or Alfredo sauce
  • Cheese (Colby, Ricotta, and parmesan)
  • Basil
  • Marzano or sun-dried tomatoes
  • Banana peppers
  • Red jalapenos (optional)
  • Vegetable seasoning
  • Italian seasoning
  • Sauce drizzle (ranch, Italian, or Special “House”)

House Flatbread Cooking Instructions

  1. Start by baking flatbread until lightly toasted
  2. Remove from oven and add tomato sauce
  3. Add toppings of choice, spread evenly
  4. Sprinkle Italian seasoning
  5. Add cheese blend, return to oven until bread is lightly browned
  6. Ad vegetable seasoning
  7. Remove from oven and add extra layer of cheese (optional)
  8. Add basil and additional topping
  9. Add sauce drizzle
