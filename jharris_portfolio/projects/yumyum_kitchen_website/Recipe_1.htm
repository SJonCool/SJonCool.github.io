Ingredients

Serving Size 2 tacos

  • Tortillas (corn, flour), or Gyro
  • Chicken, shrimp, or pork
  • cucumber
  • tomato
  • red onions
  • olives
  • spinach or kale
  • cheese (feta or gorgonzola)
  • Tzatziki
  • Jalapenos (optional)

Mediterranean Tacos Cooking Instructions

  1. In medium bowl, whisk Tzatziki with 1 tablespoon canola oil, garlic, and oregano, set aside
  2. Cook chosen meat on skillet until golden brown, add chosen vegetables
  3. Lower temperature to medium, add sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Let simmer for 4-5 addition minutes, depending on wanted texture
  6. Remove from heat, drizzle sauce over tortillas
  7. Serve with tortillas or pita bread
