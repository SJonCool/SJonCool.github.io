  • Classic Chicken Alfredo

Yum Yum Kitchen Fresh food done right!

Ingredients

Serving Size 2 cups

  • Pasta of choice
  • Parmesan sauce (or alternate blend)
  • Chicken
  • Italian seasoning
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Garlic
  • Sage

Chicken Alfredo Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook pasta of choice according to directions, drain
  2. (For additional taste, fry pasta before adding ingredients)
  3. Mix seasoning including garlic and sage in bowl
  4. Cook chicken per choice
  5. In separate bowl, mix sauce
  6. Add chicken to pasta
  7. Add basil
  8. Add seasoning to noodles and chicken
  9. Add oregano
  10. Drizzle sauce, mix well
