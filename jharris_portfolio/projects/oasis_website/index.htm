Festival

Oasis is a proud sponsor and cultivator of this year’s Woolly Worm Festival in North Carolina! A yearly event, the Woolly Worm Festival is a celebrates the folklore behind the Woolly Worm’s ability to predict the weather. With races, games, music, and plenty of fun for all ages, there’s always something to do.
